About Next Wave Cancer Rehab

Move Forward With Confidence

Cancer treatment can leave people feeling uncertain about their body — how it moves, how it responds, and what it needs next. Next Wave Cancer Rehab was founded to help people safely rebuild strength, restore movement, and regain confidence at every stage of their cancer experience.

Whether you are preparing for surgery, recovering from treatment, navigating long-term side effects, or living with advanced illness, Megan Oster provides safe, individualised physiotherapy care that meets you where you are.
