Locations

Next Wave Cancer Rehab runs sessions from two Sunshine Coast locations. Days and times are current at time of publishing and subject to change.

Cancer Care Associates

36-40 Hofmann Road, Noosaville QLD 4566

Monday 9am - 5pm

Located within the Noosa Health Precinct. The centre has a dedicated patient drop-off zone, over 150 free car parks, and the building is wheelchair accessible.

Sycamore Health

Shop 1, Level 3/1 Courage Street, Sippy Downs QLD 4556

Tuesday & Thursday 8am - 4pm

Located on Level 3 of the HQ building on Courage Street. A lift servicing the commercial levels is at the rear of the building, or use the adjacent stairwell. From Level 3, follow the signage to Sycamore Health Suites. Parking is available in the rear carpark or as 2P free parking on Courage Street.
